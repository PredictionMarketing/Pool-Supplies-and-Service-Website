Our Services

Comprehensive pool maintenance and repair solutions

Regular Maintenance

Weekly or bi-weekly pool cleaning, chemical balancing, and equipment checks to keep your pool in perfect condition.

Repairs & Troubleshooting

Expert diagnosis and repair of pumps, filters, heaters, and other pool equipment to ensure optimal performance.

Chemical Balancing

Professional water testing and chemical adjustments to maintain safe, clear, and balanced pool water.

Pool Cleaning

Thorough cleaning of pool surfaces, skimming, vacuuming, and filter maintenance to keep your pool spotless.

Opening & Closing

Seasonal pool opening and closing services to protect your investment and ensure it's ready when you are.

Leak Detection

Advanced techniques to locate and repair pool leaks, saving water and preventing structural damage.

About Us

Trusted pool professionals since 1995

Pool Supplies & Service has been providing exceptional pool maintenance and repair services to homeowners and businesses for over 25 years. Founded by Daryl Waites, our company has built a reputation for reliability, expertise, and outstanding customer service.

We understand that your pool is an investment, and we're committed to helping you protect that investment through proper maintenance and timely repairs. Our team of certified technicians has the knowledge and experience to handle all your pool service needs.

Whether you need regular maintenance, emergency repairs, or advice on pool care, we're here to help. We serve the entire local area with prompt, professional service at competitive rates.
